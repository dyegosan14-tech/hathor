About Us

Sourcing expertise between two continents.

Hathor Trading is an international sourcing and procurement company headquartered in Brazil, with operational support in Yiwu, China. We specialize in connecting manufacturers with importers by providing reliable sourcing solutions, supplier management, quality assurance, and international trade services.

Our expertise covers every stage of the procurement process, from identifying qualified manufacturers and negotiating competitive pricing to coordinating production, quality inspections, logistics, and export documentation.

By combining local market knowledge with an international perspective, we help businesses source high-quality products efficiently and confidently — building long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and operational excellence.
